Every Meal Saved Creates Another Smile.

Every day, thousands of perfectly edible meals are thrown away while many people struggle to find food. FoodSaver connects restaurants, bakeries, supermarkets, charities, and volunteers to ensure surplus food reaches those who need it instead of ending up in landfills.

The Food Waste Crisis

Why traditional donation methods fail and where the system breaks down.

Surplus & Discard

Restaurants, bakeries, and supermarkets throw away perfectly fresh, edible food daily due to overproduction or minor cosmetic flaws.

Information Gap

Charities and local food banks often have no real-time visibility into which nearby businesses have surplus food available.

Premature Expiration

Without quick dispatch, fresh baked goods and cooked meals expire before they can reach the people who need them most.

Inefficient Logistics

Traditional donation processes rely on manual phone calls, slow pickups, and lack a dedicated, organized volunteer delivery network.

Platform Core Features

Everything needed to streamline donation logistics, secure transactions, and maximize efficiency.

Secure Authentication

Role-based registration and secure login for businesses, charities, and volunteers.

Donation Management

Add list details, upload photos, set expiry timers, and manage live listings.

Live Maps

View nearby donations and active delivery routes via Google Maps integration.

Real-Time Tracking

Live GPS location updates for volunteers during pick-up and delivery stages.

QR Code Verification

Secure handovers verified by scanning QR codes at pickup and dropoff points.

Smart Notifications

Instant alerts for new requests, approval statuses, and expiration reminders.

Private Chat

Direct in-app messaging between donors, charities, and volunteers.

Analytics Dashboard

Track meals saved, CO2 reduced, and retrieve monthly environmental reports.

Ratings & Reviews

Build community trust by rating restaurants, volunteers, and charity feedback.

Reports System

Submit reports for quality issues, fake donations, or logistical complaints.

AI Smart Matching

Intelligently pair donors with the most suitable charities and volunteers.

Responsive Design

Optimized user experience on smartphones, tablets, and desktop displays.

Bilingual Support

Switch between English and Arabic layouts seamlessly at any time.

Dark & Light Mode

Choose your preferred viewing comfort with an energy-saving dark theme.

Community Rewards

AI-Powered Optimization

Leveraging machine learning to maximize efficiency and food safety.

Food Waste Prediction

AI analyzes sales patterns to predict which items are likely to remain unsold, advising businesses on quantities to donate in advance.

Charity Recommendation

Suggests the most suitable charity based on distance, capacity, urgency, and past acceptance history.

Freshness & Expiry Detection

Computer vision checks food images and cross-references expiry timers to flag potentially unsafe food.

Smart Routing & Matching

Dynamically matches restaurants, charities, and volunteers while optimization algorithms map the fastest delivery routes.
